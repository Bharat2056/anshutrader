iD888 Desktop Master

Direct Thermal

The iD888 is a Direct Thermal Barcode Printer manufactured by iDPRT. It is a compact and reliable solution designed for high-volume shipping labels, retail tags, and inventory tracking without the need for ink or toner.

Key Features

  • Printing Method: Direct Thermal (no ink/toner required)
  • Print Speed: Up to 8 inches per second (200 mm/s)
  • Resolution: 203 dpi
  • Max Print Width: 4.25″ (108mm)
  • Memory: 128 MB Flash / 32 MB RAM
  • Connectivity: Standard USB-B and Ethernet
  • Emulations: ZPL-II, TSPL, DPL, EPL2
  • Media Compatibility: Continuous, die-cut, black mark, fan-fold
